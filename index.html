Powfu Popular songs
Isaiah Faber (born March 31, 1999), better known by his stage name Powfu, is a Canadian rapper, singer, songwriter and record producer. He is the son of Dave Faber of the band Faber Drive. He rose to fame after releasing his first single, "Death Bed (Coffee for Your Head)", featuring Beabadoobee, which peaked at number 23 on the Billboard Hot 100. "Death Bed", "Snowflake", "I can't sleep", "I'll come back to you"
In February 2020, he released the song "Death Bed (Coffee for Your Head)", which samples Beabadoobee's debut single "Coffee". The single has received over 1 billion streams on Spotify as of June 2021 and peaked at number 23 on the Billboard Hot 100 after gaining popularity on the video sharing app TikTok. After re-releasing the single a year later, Powfu signed with Columbia Records in the United States, collaborating with Robots + Humans in the United Kingdom. The song appears on the EP Poems of the Past, released on May 29, 2020. On December 2, 2022, Powfu released their first full-length album, Surrounded by Hounds and Serpents, which featured a cover of Taylor Swift's "Mine". In an interview with Billboard, he said that the album's title describes his attempt to spot "people who are fake or just want [him] for the wrong reasons.""
